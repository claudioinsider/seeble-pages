About the Service

What is Seeble?
Seeble is a personalized soulmate reading and sketch service. Through a guided quiz, we analyze your preferences, personality traits, and energy to prepare a unique sketch and profile of your ideal partner.
How does the soulmate sketch work?
After completing our quiz and providing a face photo, we analyze your responses and prepare a personalized sketch along with detailed insights about your potential soulmate's traits, love style, and compatibility.
How are my readings and sketch created?
Your readings and sketch are produced by our team and automated systems based on the responses you provide through the quiz and the details in your profile.
Is this real or just for entertainment?
Our service is provided for entertainment purposes. While many users find our readings insightful and surprisingly accurate, results should not be taken as guaranteed predictions.

Account & Privacy

Is my data safe?
Yes. We use industry-standard encryption and security measures. Your payment information is processed securely by our payment provider and is never stored on our servers.
Do you sell my personal information?
No. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.
How do I delete my account?
To delete your account and all associated data, contact us at support@appseeble.com. We will process your request within 30 days.
What happens to my face photo?
Photos uploaded for the face scan feature are processed temporarily to prepare your reading and are not permanently stored on our servers.

Technical Issues

The app or quiz is not loading. What should I do?
Try closing and reopening the app, checking your internet connection, or restarting your device. If the issue persists, contact support@appseeble.com.
I didn't receive my reading. What should I do?
Give it a few moments to finish preparing. If you still haven't received it, contact support@appseeble.com with your account email and we'll look into it.
