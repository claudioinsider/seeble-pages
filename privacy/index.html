1 Introduction

Seeble ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use the Seeble mobile application.

By using Seeble, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

2 Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Email address — used for account creation and authentication
  • Name — used for your profile and personalization
  • Phone number — used for your profile (optional)
  • Profile photo — uploaded by you to personalize your account

2.2 Information We Process During App Use

  • Palm image — captured locally on your device when using the Palm Reading feature. This image is processed temporarily to generate a reading and is NOT stored on our servers.
  • Chat messages — messages you send in the AI Chat feature are processed by our AI provider (OpenAI) to generate responses. Messages are not stored permanently beyond the current session.
  • Astrological preferences — your zodiac sign, birth date, and compatibility preferences used to generate readings.
  • Soul mate preferences — gender, age range, and ethnic background preferences used to generate your soul mate image.

2.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • App usage data — which features you use, to improve the app experience.
  • Device information — operating system version and device type, for compatibility purposes.

3 How We Use Your Information

Data Purpose

4 Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the app:

4.1 Supabase

We use Supabase (supabase.com) as our backend database and storage provider. Your profile data (email, name, phone, profile photo) is stored securely on Supabase servers.

  • Supabase Privacy Policy: supabase.com/privacy

4.2 OpenAI

We use OpenAI (openai.com) to power our AI Chat feature and Palm Reading analysis. Messages sent to the AI Chat and palm reading requests are processed by OpenAI's servers.

  • OpenAI Privacy Policy: openai.com/policies/privacy-policy
  • We do not use your data to train OpenAI models (zero data retention policy applies).

5 Data Storage and Security

  • Your profile data is stored on Supabase servers with encryption in transit (TLS/HTTPS).
  • Palm reading images are processed temporarily and are NOT stored on any server.
  • Chat messages are sent to OpenAI for processing and are not permanently stored by us.
  • We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data.

6 Data Retention

Data Type Retention Period
Account data (email, name, phone) Until you delete your account
Profile photo Until you remove it or delete your account
Palm reading images Not stored — processed in real-time only
Chat messages Session-only on device; not stored on our servers
Astrological preferences Until you delete your account

7 Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data through the Edit Profile screen
  • Delete your account and all associated data through the "Delete My Account" option in the app
  • Withdraw consent at any time by deleting the app

To exercise these rights, contact us at: support@appseeble.com

8 Children's Privacy

Seeble is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.
